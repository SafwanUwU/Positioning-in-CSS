Bangladesh Traffic Jams

If you enter Dhaka traffic at 3 PM, you might come out at 6 PM… in the same spot! Some say it's the closest thing to experiencing time travel.
People in traffic have their own entertainment—eating peanuts, playing ludo, or even finishing a full cricket discussion before the signal turns green.
If honking could solve traffic jams, Bangladesh would have the world’s fastest roads. Some drivers press their horns like it’s a car’s accelerator!
Ever seen a CNG driver peacefully sleeping in traffic? Some commuters take mini naps and wake up to find themselves still in the same place.
